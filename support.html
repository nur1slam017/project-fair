Support

Common Issues

Account Recovery Issues

If you are having trouble recovering your account, make sure you have access to the email linked to your account.

Game Crashes

Ensure that your system meets the minimum requirements and that your graphics drivers are up to date.

Connection Problems

If you are experiencing connection issues, check your internet settings and ensure your firewall is not blocking the game.

Missing DLC Content

If your downloadable content is missing, try restarting the game or verifying the game files through the launcher.

Performance Issues

To improve performance, lower the graphics settings and close any background applications that may be using resources.
